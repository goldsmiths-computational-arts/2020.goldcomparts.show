Corobot: When the Coronavirus Begin to Talk

Uchan Sun

A virtual virus chatbot trained on COVID-19 related tweets and social media posts. This project aims to provide a way to communicate with the unfamiliar creature and reflect on our own.

How to interact

Just type your message and send. It might take a minute to wake the bot up, so pls be patient : ) Full sentence will get better result.
